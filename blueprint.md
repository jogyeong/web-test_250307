# GreenScape Gardening Website Blueprint

## Overview

This document outlines the design and development plan for the GreenScape Gardening company website. The goal is to create a professional, visually appealing, and user-friendly website that showcases the company's services and portfolio.

## Design and Features

### Visual Design
*   **Color Palette:** The design will use a natural color palette, including shades of green, brown, and white, to create a fresh and organic feel.
*   **Typography:** A combination of a modern sans-serif font for body text and an elegant serif font for headings will be used to ensure readability and a professional look.
*   **Layout:** The layout will be clean, spacious, and responsive, ensuring a great user experience on all devices, from desktops to smartphones.
*   **Imagery:** High-quality images of beautiful gardens, landscapes, and plants will be used throughout the site to engage users.

###- **Key Sections and Features**
*   **Header:** A sticky header containing the company logo and navigation links (Home, Services, Portfolio, Contact).
*   **Hero Section:** A full-width hero image with a catchy headline and a call-to-action button.
*   **Services Section:** A section detailing the services offered by GreenScape, such as Garden Design, Lawn Care, and Tree Surgery. Each service will be accompanied by a relevant icon and a brief description.
*   **Portfolio Section:** A gallery of past projects to showcase the quality of work.
*   **Testimonials Section:** A section with quotes from satisfied clients to build trust.
*   **Contact Section:** A simple and easy-to-use contact form, along with other contact details like phone number and email address.
*   **Footer:** The footer will include links to social media profiles, copyright information, and a back-to-top button.

## Current Plan

1.  **Structure (HTML):** Create the basic HTML structure for the website in `index.html`.
2.  **Styling (CSS):** Apply styles to the website using `style.css` to match the design specifications.
3.  **Interactivity (JavaScript):** Add interactivity to the website using `main.js`, such as a smooth scrolling effect for navigation links.
